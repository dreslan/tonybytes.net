<strong>Tragic, thought provoking, life changing</strong><br/><br/>I cried for Paul and for Lucy multiple times while reading Paul's words, and yet I couldn't put the book down. This book has the potential to change the way you think about your own mortality - it is an emotionally and morally tough read, but also, it is filled with meaning, love, and entertaining stories that shed light on the crazy and grueling world of a doctor, and also stories that teach.
